Episode summary: Zo&euml; Schlanger is an author, journalist, and current staff writer at the Atlantic, where she covers the newsletter &ldquo;The Weekly Planet&rdquo;. Schlanger has written for major outlets such as Newsweek, Quartz, Wired, The New York Times, The Nation, Time Magazine, and NPR. Schlanger is also the author of the 2024 book The Light Eaters: How the Unseen World of Plant Intelligence Offers a New Understanding of Life on Earth. Her work focuses on science and environment- in particular climate change, pollution, and environmental justice.
